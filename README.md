# Public-Relations
Public Relations of AguaClara focuses on increasing overall exposure of the project team within Cornell and the global community.

Public Relations creates and manages a consistent brand image that communicate AguaClara Cornellâ€™s innovative and sustainable ideals. Internal team pride and campus exposure is increased by designing team merchandise, maintaining regular social media presence, and overseeing recruitment materials.
